Location

One&Only Reethi Rah is one of the largest islands in the North Malé Atoll. The resort is a 45-minute transfer from Velana International Airport or a 15-minute seaplane journey.

families

One&Only Maldives at Reethi Rah welcomes families with children of all ages and offers the following facilities and services to ensure they have an enjoyable stay.

  • There are family rooms, two villa residences, 2-bedroom grand beach villa and 3-bedroom grand sunset residence.
  • Baby cots free of charge
  • Bottle sterilisers, warmers, and monitors in guest rooms can be requested
  • Baby Sets - toilet adapters, bath, and crawling basket can be requested
  • Minibar can be emptied on request as well as alcoholic beverages
  • Children’s menu is available at all restaurants
  • Highchairs are available
  • Offers/sells baby food in restaurants
  • No restricted access for children in any restaurants
  • Medical assistance is available, and charges apply
  • There is a doctor onsite or on-call
  • There is a shop for baby goods and supplies called NEO Kidswear Shop
  • There are nappy changing facilities in public toilets
  • Child car seats are not fitted in hotel vehicles
  • Other amenities - When you book a family villa you will get a complimentary buggy for the duration of your stay.
  • Children’s pool
  • Beach and sea suitable for young children
  • There is shade available

  • There is a lifeguard on duty
